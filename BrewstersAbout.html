About Brewsters

Brewers Coffee Co. is a locally owned business right in the heart of Glendale, CA.

Brewers opened in 2006, with the vision of bringing a gourmet coffee shop with a positive atmosphere to the city of Glendale, CA. We have a little bit of everything to offer anyone who comes!

Join us for our monthly art shows, every third thursday right here in the coffee shop. We also have local artists and bands performing regularly on weekends, from right here in the Los Angeles Area!
